### Acceleration Engine
_New flagship AI surface (2026-06-30 brainstorm). Mines the user's observed work — `ActivitySession[]`, `WorkBlock[]`, calendar load, and the correction trail — into evidence-cited **Plays**: AUTOMATE (a generated, reusable AI "skill" recipe grounded in the user's real projects/tools), TOOL (a tool recommendation matched to an observed time-sink), and TECHNIQUE (a trick tied to an observed anti-pattern). Two layers, mirroring the Forecast Agent: a **deterministic miner** in `packages/inference/` that produces ranked signals with full evidence + estimated time saved (runs locally, no AI, always on, privacy-safe), and an **optional opt-in AI synthesis** layer that authors the polished Plays. The AI call routes through the EXISTING `ai_complete` Tauri command (`services/aiComplete.ts`) so the whole feature is loop-safe — NO Rust work. Hard privacy rule: the deterministic miner may read raw window titles locally, but the AI layer sends **derived signals only** (app-name sequences, category/duration/time-of-day stats, counts) — never raw window titles — gated and audited like every other AI command. Build on the established patterns: `forecastAgentPrompt.ts`/`useForecastAgent.ts` for the AI flow, the 4-file `AuditEventType` recipe for audit, the `Screen`/`ScreenRouter`/`ui.ts` trio for the new surface, and the "demo seed must exercise every flagship visual distinctly" convention for the wow.
