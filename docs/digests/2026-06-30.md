### Capacity Model (3)

- **Target-utilization model** (#148, `capacity.ts` · `models.ts` · `WeeklyCapacityScreen.tsx` · `demoData.ts`) — replaced "100% minus penalties, clamp 0–40%" with a principled 80%-knee model. Forward-committed load (`recurring + carryover + reactive×0.72 + fragmentation + wip`) is now summed into a new `committed_utilization_pct` snapshot field; reliable new work = `clamp(80 − committed, 0, 40)`, bringing total utilization to the M/M/1 ~80% knee instead of filling to 100%. `MAX_RELIABLE_NEW_WORK = 40` kept as a guardrail; narrative + hero-card copy updated to cite the committed-utilization story.

- **WIP penalty super-linear** (#147, `capacity.ts`) — replaced linear `uniqueProjectCount / 10` with quadratic `clamp(pow(activeProjectCount / 7, 2), 0, 1)`. Cost now curves upward at high project counts, saturating the penalty at 7 concurrent projects ("badly overloaded" knee); sits near the old value at 5 projects, gentler below, harsher above. Verified super-linear and NaN-safe.

- **Document capacity constants with citations** (#141, `capacity.ts` comments only) — `MAX_RELIABLE_NEW_WORK`, the `×0.55` carry discount, the `×0.72` reactive penalty, and the `context_switch_score ≥ 0.45` fragmentation trigger now each point to `docs/heuristics-vs-research.md` and name their empirical basis. No behavior change.
